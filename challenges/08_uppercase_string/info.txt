DESCRIPTION: Uppercase the string contents — "hello world" becomes "HELLO WORLD"
OPTIMAL: 4
SOLUTION_A: gUi"  —  uppercase operator on inside-quotes text object (g U i " = 4 keys)
SOLUTION_B: f"lvt"U  —  find quote, visual select, uppercase (more keys, less idiomatic)
NOTE: gU is the uppercase operator. It works with any text object or motion: gUiw (word), gUi{ (block), gUU (whole line). No need to enter insert mode.
