DESCRIPTION: Wrap the expression "price + shipping" in Math.round(...)
OPTIMAL: 23
SOLUTION_A: f=wiMath.round(<Esc>f;i)<Esc>ZZ  —  find =, word-forward to price, insert before it; find ;, insert ) before it
NOTE: Two insertions at precise positions. f=w positions cursor at start of the expression. The second f; finds the semicolon so you can insert the closing paren before it.
