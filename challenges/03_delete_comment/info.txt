DESCRIPTION: Delete the trailing comment, leave "const value = 42;"
OPTIMAL: 4
SOLUTION_A: f/hD  —  find '/', move left to space, delete to EOL (4 keys)
SOLUTION_B: f;a <BS><Esc>... no — worse
NOTE: f/ lands ON the slash. h moves to the space before it. D deletes space + comment. Clean result with no trailing space.
NOTE: Alternative: T;D — T; goes backward-till-semicolon, landing on space after it, then D. Same keystroke count but elegant if cursor is past the semicolon.
